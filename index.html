Youhua Guo

Our beloved mother and grandmother, Youhua Guo, passed away peacefully at age 96 in San Mateo, California on Monday, January 1, 2024.

Memorial Service Details

Please join us on Saturday, January 13, 2024 at 10am for a memorial service to celebrate Youhua’s life. The service will be held at Woodlawn Memorial Park in Colma, CA. The burial service will follow at 11am. Lunch will be served at 12:30pm at the Noodle Shop in San Mateo.
